About Us

Our Mission

Our most valuable assets are the teams that we compete alongside with. The connections we make through our journey help define us and indirectly, the teams around us. Wolves Robotics’ mission is to show the world what is possible when everyone works together in a big community filled with great minds, and how gracious professionalism optimistically affects all teams! We know that when we strive for great things together, our community becomes stronger and we’re more inspiring to others.

Our Team

We are Wolves Robotics, a community team from San Antonio, Texas. Although this is most of our members' first-third year on the team, our team number has a 13-year history, dating back to when it was established in 2011. After being inactive for multiple years, Wolves Robotics was revived in 2022. Since then, we’ve been committed to making the most of this fresh start. As a community team, we selectively bring in members based on their passion, skills, and contributions.
